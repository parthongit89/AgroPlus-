A. SOIL HEALTH FIRST – "Feed the Soil, Not Just the Crop"

  1. Soil Testing – Do a lab soil test every 2 years.
    • Check: pH, organic carbon, NPK, micronutrients (Zn, Fe, B).
    • Get free kits or ₹10/sample testing via soil health card scheme.
  2. Organic Carbon Boosters:
    • o Add FYM (2–3 tons/acre) + vermicompost (500–800 kg/acre).
    • Add biochar or crop residue compost – improves water holding by 25%.
  3. Biofertilizers:
    • Mix Azospirillum + PSB + Rhizobium + Trichoderma in FYM before application.
  4. Green Manuring:
    • Grow dhaincha, sunhemp, cowpea before main crop — plough into soil for natural nitrogen.

B. ORGANIC + TECH BASED PEST MANAGEMENT (IPM)

  1. Pheromone Traps:
    • Use for fruit borer, shoot borer, armyworm — 10–12 traps/acre.
  2. Light Traps:
    • Solar or battery-based traps to kill night moths — 2 per acre.
  3. Neem Products:
    • Use Neem oil 3–5% or NSKE 5% at 7–10 day intervals.
  4. Biocontrol Agents:
    • Trichoderma harzianum (for soil diseases)
    • Pseudomonas fluorescens (for bacterial wilt)
    • Bacillus thuringiensis (for caterpillars).
  5. Crop Rotation:
    • Avoid planting same family crop (like tomato after brinjal) — breaks pest cycle.
