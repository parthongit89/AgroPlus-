#  AgroPlus+

**AgroPlus+** is a smart agriculture platform designed to support farmers with essential tools for crop management, market awareness, and decision-making. The project aims to bring multiple agricultural services together in a single, easy-to-use platform.

##  Project Overview

Farmers often face challenges such as:

- Scattered agricultural information
- Lack of real-time decision support
- Limited access to unified farming tools
- Network connectivity issues in rural areas
- Difficulty tracking farm records and crop health

AgroPlus+ is designed to address these challenges through a simple and farmer-friendly digital solution.

---

##  Key Features

###  Agro Market
- Daily agricultural market prices
- Market trends and updates
- Better decision-making for selling crops

###  Agro Chat
- Communication with agricultural experts
- Farmer-to-farmer discussions
- Knowledge sharing and community support

###  Agro Records
- Digital farm record management
- Crop activity tracking
- Expense and productivity monitoring

###  iCaptured – Plant Infection Detector
- AI-assisted crop disease detection
- Image-based plant health analysis
- Early disease identification support

###  Pest Radar
- Pest monitoring and alert system
- Early warning notifications
- Improved crop protection planning
